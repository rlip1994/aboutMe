story

Every human is an artist. And this is the main art that we have:
the creation of our story.
-Miguel Ruiz

My names is Alex André Solís Barrantes, I was born December 31st, 1994. At the age of 2 I was diagnosed with polyneuropathy, but it never stopped me. It taught me how to see the world in a different way.

When I was 4 years old, I touched my first computer and I had a lot of questions. My teacher said, “this machine is a magic box, you can create a world with knowledge, imagination and math”. Seconds later, I knew my calling, I wanted to create that kind of magic.

5 years later, I created my first program, a “hello world” in BASIC language. I learned BASIC with “Game Programming for Teens” but this book is so hard that i don't recommended it to start, however; i used it to improve myself and it was my first weapon to start becoming a programmer.

At the age of 16 I did my first Motivational speech, I was so nervous, but my fear didn't stop me, I went out and did my best. They were students with 1000 eyes looking at me. Ever since I have been learning so many things from those speeches and it helps me grow as a human being.

December 31st, 2010 Is my birthday and i start to worry about my career, I want to work on something that i love. For instance, I like math, I like to solve problems and this is my perfect career: software engineering. I studied as never before to be admitted at the Instituto Tecnológico de Costa Rica, and i succeed.

When I was 22 years old I started in my first job, developer of Cyber Security software at Intel. I learned so many things about Cyber Security and other technologies like C++, angular, TC. Here they help me with physical therapy and did everything necessary for my needs to be met comfortably. In May of 2018 I moved to work in the area of innovation at Intel.
